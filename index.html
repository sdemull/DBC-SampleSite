Our Story

Built for Players,
By Players

The DeWitt Batting Center was created to give Mid-Michigan athletes a dedicated space to develop their skills year-round — rain, snow, or shine. Whether you're a 7-year-old picking up a bat for the first time or a high schooler trying to earn a college roster spot, this is your facility.

  • Baseball and softball cages — separate equipment for each
  • Clean, climate-controlled indoor environment
  • Flexible hours to fit school and practice schedules
  • Friendly, knowledgeable staff on-site
  • Located in DeWitt Township — easy access from the Lansing area
Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

Yes, players should bring their own bat and batting helmet. We recommend bringing your own batting gloves as well. Balls are provided for machine-pitch cages.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24–48 hours ahead, especially for weekend slots which fill quickly. Same-day booking is available based on cage availability.

What ages are the cages appropriate for?

We welcome players of all ages — from tee-ballers to adults. Cage settings and pitching machine speeds can be adjusted to fit any skill level.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes. Cancellations and reschedules are accepted up to 24 hours before your session. Please use the link in your confirmation email or contact us directly.

Do you offer lessons for beginners?

Absolutely. Our instructors work with players at every level, including complete beginners. Private lessons are a great way to build a strong foundation early.
